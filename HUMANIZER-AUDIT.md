# Site-wide humanizer audit

Date: 2026-07-21

## Scope

Reviewed the public marketing and editorial copy in `app/page.tsx`, `app/data.ts`, `app/components.tsx`, `app/layout.tsx`, `app/contact/page.tsx`, `app/thank-you/page.tsx`, `app/blog/page.tsx`, and `app/blog/[slug]/page.tsx`.

## What changed

- Made the Philippines-only hiring model clear on the homepage, contact flow, guide index, article template, shared CTA, footer, and metadata.
- Replaced broad global and country-comparison language with Filipino talent, Philippines-based shifts, and practical handoff decisions.
- Removed unsupported setup times, task counts, work-hour ranges, and dormant proof-style figures.
- Repaired awkward copy left by earlier edits, including a broken pricing sentence and vague staffing labels.
- Replaced filler and placeholder language with plain task, access, review, and approval guidance.
- Tightened repeated template copy while keeping route slugs, schema types, citations, SEO intent, and conversion paths intact.
- Restored consistent spacing between guide cards and made source links visibly identifiable after local detail-page QA.

## Final anti-AI pass

The remaining copy uses short sentences, specific work examples, and direct buyer questions. It avoids significance claims, corporate filler, forced contrasts, em-dash-heavy prose, vague authority claims, and generic upbeat conclusions.

## Exclusions

Privacy, terms, cancellation, and cancellation-policy pages were excluded. Their legal meaning was not changed. No testimonials, first-person stories, statistics, credentials, rates, or performance claims were added.
